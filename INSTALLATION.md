# Installation Instructions for MDH TV Streaming App on Android Devices

## Prerequisites
Before you start the installation process, ensure that your Android device meets the following requirements:
- Android version: 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher
- Sufficient storage space (at least 100 MB free)
- Internet connection (Wi-Fi or mobile data)

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Enable Installation from Unknown Sources
1. Open the **Settings** app on your Android device.
2. Scroll down and tap on **Security** or **Privacy**.
3. Find the option labeled **Unknown Sources** and toggle it to **Enable**. This allows you to install apps from sources other than the Google Play Store.

### Step 2: Download the MDH TV Streaming App
1. Open your preferred web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox).
2. Visit the official website or the direct link provided to download the MDH TV Streaming App APK file. (Replace the link with wherever the app can be downloaded)
3. Tap on the download link to start downloading the APK file. Wait for the download to complete.

### Step 3: Locate and Install the APK File
1. Once the download is complete, open the **Downloads** folder from your file manager or swipe down from the top of the screen and tap on the downloaded file.
2. If prompted, tap **Install** to start the installation process.
3. Wait for the installation to finish. You may be asked to grant permissions that the app requires. 
4. Once installed, you can find the **MDH TV Streaming App** icon in your app drawer.

### Step 4: Launch the App
1. Locate the MDH TV Streaming App icon on your device and tap it to open.
2. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your account or sign in.

### Step 5: Troubleshooting
- If you encounter any issues during installation, make sure that:
  - Your device has enough storage space.
  - You have a stable internet connection.
  - The Unknown Sources option is still enabled.

## Conclusion
You have now successfully installed the MDH TV Streaming App on your Android device! Enjoy streaming your favorite content!
