# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## What is MDH TV Streaming?
MDH TV Streaming is a service that allows you to watch your favorite shows and movies online!

## How much does it cost?
The service costs $9.99 per month with a free trial for the first month.

## What devices can I use to access MDH TV Streaming?
You can use smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, and web browsers to access our content.

## Can I download content to watch offline?
Yes, you can download certain titles to watch offline on supported devices.

## How many devices can I use simultaneously?
You can stream on up to three devices at the same time with one subscription.

## How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription anytime in the account settings under "Subscription".

If you have any more questions or need assistance, please contact our support team!
